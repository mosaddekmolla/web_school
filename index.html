About Lions School and College

Details About Lions School and College

Lions School And College is an academic institute located at Officices coloni cantorman road Saidpur Nilphamari. Its institute code (EIIN) is 125204.

Lions schools & college are committed to provide intellectual, social, cultural and economic benefits to communities through programs of education, co-curriculum activities, research and scholarships. Our schools & colleges are delivering world class education and research in the national education system of Bangladesh.

Moreover, it has been involved in the next practice in the form of world citizenship education and makes an innovative and creative system for smart and enlightened generation.Our mission is to produce highly skilled manpower, visionary leaders and enlightened citizens..
